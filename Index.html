Experience


I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Design and Fine Arts from Makerere University in Uganda.
Afterward, I completed a short course in Fundamentals of Digital Marketing from Google Courses.


I embarked on my creative and marketing journey in 2019 as an intern at Maad McCain Advertising.
Over the next two years, I climbed the ranks to become a Digital Account Manager. From 2021 to 2023, I furthered my career as a Digital Lead at Clever Box Creations.

My stay at Clever Box allowed me to venture deeper into the creative and communications world.

Now, I serve my customers and clients with unwavering confidence and a wealth of professional expertise in the realms of my aforementioned skills as a creative artist.
